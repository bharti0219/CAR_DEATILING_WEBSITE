PAINT CORRECTIONS

What is a Paint Correction?



Paint correction is the specialized process of restoring and rejuvenating the paintwork of a vehicle, mostly through the elimination of surface scratches and imperfections.

At SUKH CAR DETAILING's, we can distinguish between paintwork that has been truly corrected and paintwork that has been treated with products that are designed to mask and fill surface imperfections.

From swirl marks, fine and deep scratches, paint fading, oxidation, and more – we have you covered. We use some of the industry-leading tools, products, and techniques to ensure 100% customer satisfaction and quality care.









































STEP 1 : PREPARE THE SURFACE




  • SCD’s Meticulous Exterior Strip Wash
  • Removal of Bugs, Road Contaminants or any previous Wax/Sealants
  • Full Exterior Iron Decontamination Wash
  • Full Exterior Tar Decontamination Wash
  • Full Exterior Clay Bar
  • Rim Decontamination & Brake Dust Removal
  • Interior and Exterior Windows Steamed & Shined Streak-Free








STEP 2: POLISH & RESTORE




1-STAGE CORRECTION

Single Stage Polish to Remove Surface Oxidation, Fine Scratching & Restore Color Depth
6-Month Polymer Sealant Included

2-STAGE CORRECTION

Two-Stage Cut & Polish to Remove Surface Swirls & Deeper Scratching, Oxidation & Restore Color Depth
6-Month Polymer Sealant Included

3-STAGE CORRECTION

Multi-Stage Correction to Restore Paint Paint Undergoes 3-Unique Stages of Sanding, Cut & Final Polish
6-Month Polymer Sealant Included
